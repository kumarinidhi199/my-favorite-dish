Gulab jamun is a calorie-rich dessert.It is a milk-solid based sweet from the Indian continent and a type of mithai,popular in India,Nepal,The Maldives and Bangladesh. It is made mainly from milk solids,traditionally from khoya,which is a milk reduced to the consistency of a soft dough.Modern recipes call for dried or powdered milk instead of khoya.It is often garnished with dried nuts such as almonds to enhance flavour.

I like it most as jamuns are low calorie,which makes them perfect healthy snacks.I can able to make it at home with some ingredients.
